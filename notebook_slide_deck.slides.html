Introduction¶

Bay Wheels is a regional public bicycle sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area, California operated by Motivate in a partnership with the Metropolitan Transportation Commission and the Bay Area Air Quality Management District. Bay Wheels is the first regional and large-scale bicycle sharing system deployed in California and on the West Coast of the United States. It was established as Bay Area Bike Share in August 2013. As of January 2018, the Bay Wheels system had over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose.

plt.figure(figsize=[16, 8]);

plt.subplot(2, 1, 1)
customers = df.query('user_type == "Customer"')
ct_counts = customers.groupby(['start_day_of_week', 'start_hour_of_day']).size()
ct_counts = ct_counts.reset_index(name='count')
ct_counts = ct_counts.pivot(index='start_day_of_week', columns='start_hour_of_day', values='count')
sns.heatmap(ct_counts, cmap='YlGnBu',fmt='d', annot=True);
plt.title('Customer', loc='center');
plt.xlabel('Hour of Day');
plt.ylabel('Day of Week');
plt.tight_layout();

plt.subplot(2, 1, 2)
subscribers = df.query('user_type == "Subscriber"')
st_counts = subscribers.groupby(['start_day_of_week', 'start_hour_of_day']).size()
st_counts = st_counts.reset_index(name='count')
st_counts = st_counts.pivot(index='start_day_of_week', columns='start_hour_of_day', values='count')
sns.heatmap(st_counts, cmap='YlGnBu',fmt='d', annot=True);
plt.title('Subscriber', loc='center');
plt.xlabel('Hour of Day');
plt.ylabel('Day of Week');
plt.tight_layout();
plt.suptitle('Hourly Usage during Weekdays for Customers and Subscribers', fontsize=14, fontweight='semibold', y=1.1);

Result:

  • Here the visualisation predict that 8 AM in the morning and 5 PM in the evening the peak time when users hiring bike Trips.
  • Morning 7-9 Am and Evening from 04-06 Am is the rush hour for the bike trips
  • Users hire trips mostly on workdays.
